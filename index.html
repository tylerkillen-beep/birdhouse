Our Story

What Is
The Birdhouse?

The Birdhouse is a fully student-run coffee shop at Nixa High School, operating since 2014. What started as a small classroom project has grown into a real business serving the Nixa community every day — from hand-crafted drinks to classroom deliveries. Every latte poured, every order managed, and every shift worked is handled by NHS students who show up, work hard, and take pride in what they build together.

More than a coffee shop, the Birdhouse is a classroom without walls. Students gain hands-on experience in customer service, inventory management, food and beverage preparation, marketing, and real-world business operations — all while earning class credit. It's the kind of experience that sticks with you long after graduation, giving students a genuine edge when they step into college and careers.
